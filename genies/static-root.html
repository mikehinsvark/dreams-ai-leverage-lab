SYS/03Agent Architecture

Built like a real employee—across five layers.

Every genie has a personality, a job description, a boss it knows personally, a memory, and a schedule.

LayerSystem ComponentOperating Rule
  1. 01

    Soul File

    The personality

    Mike’s voice: confident, warm, zero hype. No corporate fluff. Never says “recruit”—everybody wants a strategic alliance. Every word earns its place.

  2. 02

    Identity & Lane

    The job description

    One specialist per lane, with hard guardrails: honesty rules, no invented numbers, and compensation questions route to the upline. Genies hand off to the right sibling instead of guessing.

  3. 03

    User File

    It knows you

    Your market, your goals, and your book of business—plus your writing voice captured from 50 real sent emails. Every draft sounds like you, down to the sign-off: “Oh by the way, I am never too busy for your referrals.”

  4. 04

    Genie Journal

    Shared team memory

    When a script works, an objection appears, or a market shifts, the genie saves the lesson. All four genies share one journal—a win for the Sales Genie makes the Partnership Genie smarter.

  5. 05

    Heartbeats

    It works while you sleep

    4:30 AM: the morning briefing builds your Top 3 Signals from your live calendar and inbox. Friday at 4 PM: the Assess loop reviews the whole week, celebrates wins, flags stalls, and writes lessons into the Journal.
