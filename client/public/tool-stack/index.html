NotebookLM
Learning Engine
Introduced Week 7
✓ Free from Google
"Turn your documents into podcasts, quizzes, and a personal tutor that only knows YOUR business."
NotebookLM is Google's AI research and learning tool. Upload your carrier guides, contracts, training recordings, or any documents — and it creates Audio Overviews (podcasts), quizzes, flashcards, and a personal tutor that only knows what you've uploaded. It's the fastest way to become the expert in any product or market.
  • Audio Overviews — learn carrier guides and contracts as podcasts in the car
  • Flashcards and quizzes from your own documents — study smarter, not harder
  • Personal tutor that only knows YOUR business materials — not generic internet info
  • Completely free from Google — no paid upgrade needed

Claude Cowork
The Doer
Introduced Week 8
"Builds real documents, spreadsheets, and decks — and runs tasks on a schedule."
Claude Cowork is Claude's agentic mode — it doesn't just answer questions, it actually does work. It builds real documents, spreadsheets, and presentations, works with your actual files, and can run scheduled tasks automatically. The most powerful use: a daily morning briefing that runs every morning before you wake up.
  • Automatic morning briefings — wake up to a summary of your day, priorities, and pipeline

  • Introduced Week 8 — after you're already getting results with the free tools

Manus
The Delegator
Introduced Week 9
✓ Free Credits to Start
"Hand it whole projects and get back finished work — reports, decks, simple pages."

  • Reports, decks & pages from a single prompt — not just text output
